Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Download the LSTD app from the App Store, tap "Sign Up", and follow the prompts. You can sign up with email, Google, or Apple.
Q: Is LSTD free to use?
A: Yes! LSTD is free to download and use. We offer optional VIP and Member subscriptions for exclusive benefits.

Guest Lists

Q: How do I join a guest list?
A: Browse venues, select an event, tap "Join Guest List", fill in your details, and submit. You'll receive a QR code once approved.
Q: How do I know if I'm approved?
A: You'll receive a notification when your guest list request is approved. Check "My Guest Lists" in your profile.
Q: Can I bring guests?
A: Yes, specify the number of guests when joining the list. All guests must be 21+.

Table Bookings

Q: How do table bookings work?
A: Select a venue, tap "Book a Table", choose your date and table, then complete checkout. Some tables require a deposit.
Q: What is a minimum spend?
A: The minimum amount you must spend on food and drinks at the venue. This varies by table and venue.
Q: How do I cancel a booking?
A: Contact the venue directly or email support@glist.app. Cancellation policies vary by venue.

Event Tickets

Q: How do I buy tickets?
A: Browse events, select ticket type, choose quantity, and complete checkout. Your QR code ticket will appear in "My Tickets".
Q: Can I get a refund?
A: Ticket sales are final. Refunds are only available in exceptional circumstances and subject to venue policies.

Social Features

Q: How do I find friends?
A: Tap the "Find Friends" button in your profile, search by name or username, and tap "Follow".
Q: How does "Who's Going" work?
A: When viewing a venue, you'll see which of your friends have bookings or are on the guest list for that venue.

VIP & Member Tiers

Q: What are the benefits of VIP?
A: VIP members get priority guest list access, exclusive event invites, special promotions, and premium support.
Q: How do I upgrade?
A: Tap your tier badge in your profile, select VIP or Member, and complete the subscription.
Q: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes, anytime through your account settings. You'll retain benefits until the end of your billing period.

Map & Location

Q: Why does LSTD need my location?
A: To show you nearby venues and display your location on the map. You can disable this in settings.
Q: What is the heatmap?
A: Venues with more activity (friends, bookings) appear "hotter" on the map, helping you find popular spots.

Payments

Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes, all payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never store your card details.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay.

Account & Privacy

Q: How do I delete my account?
A: Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
Q: How is my data used?
A: Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about data collection and usage.

Troubleshooting

Q: The app isn't loading properly
A: Try these steps:
  • Close and restart the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Update to the latest version
  • Restart your device
Q: I didn't receive my QR code
A: Check your "My Guest Lists" or "My Tickets" section. If still missing, contact support@glist.app.

Still Need Help?

If you can't find the answer to your question, please email us at support@glist.app with:

  • Your account email
  • A description of the issue
  • Screenshots (if applicable)
  • Device type and iOS version

We typically respond within 24 hours during business hours.

# Privacy Policy for Glist

**Last updated: November 20, 2025**

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Glist ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our application and tell you about your privacy rights and how the law protects you.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

- **Guest List Information**: Name, Email Address, Date of Reservation, and Number of Guests. This information is stored locally on your device and is only used to simulate guest list submissions.
- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage data to improve our services.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- Process your guest list requests.
- Manage your favorite venues locally on your device.

## 4. Data Storage

All personal data (Name, Email, Favorites) is stored locally on your device using Apple's `UserDefaults`. We do not transmit this data to any external server.

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our app may include links to third-party websites (e.g., Apple Maps). Clicking on those links may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We do not control these third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy statements.
